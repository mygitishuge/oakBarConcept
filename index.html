Old Oak

In the heart of Bar city, around a corner, situated in an easily missed alley way, exists Old Oak pub. This legendary establishment has existed longer than anyone can remember and has hosted some of the worlds biggest drinkers. It is rumoured that the pub was started by the very first Irishman ever to neck a beer...but that's all speculation at this point.
